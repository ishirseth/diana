CAMS

CAMS stands for Cat Adoption Management System — a mobile-first rescue workflow platform designed to help animal welfare organizations manage cat profiles, adoption requests, internal coordination, and placement progress in one structured system.

Mobile-first Built for mediators and rescue teams working on the go.

Purpose

To make rescue mediation more transparent, trackable, and efficient by replacing scattered communication and manual follow-up with a clearer digital workflow.

What CAMS is about

A digital workflow built around rescue reality

CAMS is designed for organizations that manage cat adoption across multiple communication channels, external listing platforms, and volunteer-led coordination steps.

📸

Cat album management

Create structured cat profiles with photos, videos, bio-data, descriptions, and a permanent record of adoption activity.

💬

Internal communication

Keep follow-up messages, files, links, and mediator coordination attached to the right cat case instead of scattered across chats and emails.

✅

Adoption progress tracking

Guide each case from availability to request, reservation, and adoption with clear statuses and operational checklists.

Core workflow

How the CAMS MVP works

The MVP follows the real process rescue teams use today and brings it into one connected product flow.

End-to-end adoption journey

  • 1Mediators log in and create a cat album with media, details, and adoption-ready information.
  • 2The cat can be published to external platforms and shared through social media channels.
  • 3The first incoming request is captured and linked directly to the cat record.
  • 4All internal follow-up happens inside the system, including attachments and communication history.
  • 5Application review, homecheck result, reservation steps, contract handling, and final adoption are tracked in one place.

Status flow

  • 🐾Available — the cat is ready to be listed and matched.
  • 📩Request — an interested adopter has entered the process.
  • 🔒Reserved — checks are positive and operational preparation is underway.
  • 🏡Adopted — the placement is completed and recorded.
  • 🗂️Unavailable / Archived — used for cats no longer active in the adoption pipeline.
Project brief

Capstone summary

This project combines product thinking, delivery planning, workflow design, and software project structure for a nonprofit-oriented rescue use case.

Project objective

Build an MVP for a rescue mediation platform that centralizes cat profile management, request intake, internal communication, and adoption progress tracking for animal welfare organizations.

  • 🎯Replace fragmented spreadsheets, inboxes, and chat-based coordination with one structured workflow.
  • 🤝Support mediators and rescue teams with better visibility, consistency, and case ownership.
  • 🌍Enable external publishing and cross-channel communication in a more manageable process.

Delivery focus

Discovery & planning Define users, process pain points, requirements, and MVP priorities.
Design & setup Prepare UX/UI concepts, technical foundations, backlog structure, and project workflow.
Build & validate Develop the product flow, test key scenarios, and validate usability with realistic rescue operations.
Launch & stabilization Support deployment, training, and post-launch monitoring for the MVP release.
